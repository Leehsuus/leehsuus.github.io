Powerful Tools. Seamless Integration.

Everything you need to organize your reading, built directly into the AO3 interface you already know and love.

Reading Status & Progress FREE

Never lose your place again. Mark works as Read, Partially Read, or Do Not Read directly from the blurb or the work page itself.

  • Visual tags on blurbs
  • Chapter-level progress tracking
  • Automatic native AO3 bookmark sync

Text Highlights & Notes PRO

Select any text to highlight it in vibrant colors. Write private chapter reviews, rate chapters with stars, and view all your annotations in your personal Notebook.

  • Floating color menu with dark/light mode support
  • Scrollbar minimap to easily locate annotations
  • Searchable Notebook dashboard

Want To Read Queue PRO

Build your reading list effortlessly. Click the bookmark icon on any blurb to add it to your queue, accessible instantly from the AO3 header dropdown anywhere on the site.

  • One-click queueing from search results
  • Auto-cleanup when a work is marked as read
  • Dedicated WTR filter for your bookmarks page

Google Drive Sync FREE

Never lose your reading history. Automatically backup your reading status, settings, highlights, and reviews to your personal Google Drive or JSONBin.

  • Secure OAuth 2.0 connection
  • Cross-device synchronization
  • 100% private. We don't host any of your data.

Seamless Cloud Backup

Sync securely to your personal Drive.

Bookmark Folder Grouping PRO

Clean up your massive bookmark list. Automatically group your AO3 bookmarks into beautiful, color-coded collapsible folders sorted by Fandom.

  • Custom folder colors
  • Collapsible UI for better navigation

Support the Project & Unlock PRO

AO3 Advanced Marker is built with love by a solo developer. By supporting the project with a one-time purchase, you get a lifetime license to all premium features and help keep the extension ad-free and tracking-free forever.
