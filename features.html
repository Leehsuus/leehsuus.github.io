Extension Features

Explore all the tools designed to elevate your AO3 reading experience, completely privacy-first.

Free Features

Reading Status Tracking

Mark works as Read, track your Chapter-by-Chapter progress, or mark works as "Do Not Read". Visual badges and background colors easily highlight your progress directly on AO3 listings.

Chapter Reviews & Notebook

Write personal reviews and notes for individual chapters directly on the reading page. View and manage all your notes centrally in a dedicated Notebook dashboard in the options menu.

Star Ratings

Rate chapters from 1 to 5 stars. The extension automatically calculates and displays the overall average rating for the whole work on blurb listings to help you remember your favorites.

Reading Statistics

Track your reading habits with detailed stats! View your total words read, yearly book goals, reading streaks, and charts showcasing your top fandoms and most-read authors.

Infinite Scroll

Say goodbye to clicking "Next". Seamlessly and infinitely scroll through search pages, tags, and bookmarks with automatic next-page loading (fully configurable and can be disabled at any time).

Active Reading Filter

Tired of seeing fics you've already read? Automatically hide works you've marked as "Read" or "Do Not Read" from search results with a single click, keeping your listings fresh.

Native AO3 Bookmark Sync

Automatically sync your extension's "Read" status directly to your private AO3 bookmarks, and keep your native bookmarks page auto-sorted by the latest update date.

Cloud Backup & Sync

Securely back up your reading progress, stats, and reviews using Google Drive or JSONBin, allowing seamless synchronization and data recovery across all your devices.

Custom Colors

Fully personalize the extension's badges and buttons. Choose your favorite hex colors via the options panel to match your aesthetic and dark modes.

PRO Features

Unlock these premium features by supporting the development of the extension.

👑 'Want to Read' Queue

Save works for later with a single click. Keep your 'Want to Read' list neatly organized and accessible via a quick dropdown menu in the header, entirely separated from your regular bookmarks.

👑 Text Highlights

Highlight your favorite quotes directly in the text using multiple custom colors. Find your highlights effortlessly with a scrollbar minimap and manage them all in the Highlights dashboard.

👑 Bookmark Folder Grouping

Organize your messy bookmarks into collapsible, color-coded folders grouped by fandom, transforming your bookmark page into a beautifully structured and easy-to-navigate library.
